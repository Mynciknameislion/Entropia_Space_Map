Skyflails are magnificent long distance travellers. There is no other creature that travels further during its lifespan. The reason behind the Skyflail's wanderlust remains a mystery, however breeding is believed to be a large part of it. Each mature Skyflail accounts for over a thousand offspring. If not for the fact that many succumb to predation or disease, humans would likely be facing a silent universal invasion.

The astronautically horrific Locusta creature resembles a ghastly hybrid between a wasp and lobster. This appearance disguises a keen intelligence which rivals that of humans. Disturbingly, this intelligence seems to have developed in conjuction with a singular purpose in life: to wander the galaxy and devour everything in its path, biological or mineral.

Truly a freak of nature, the Dymlek possesses unique powers of rejuvenation. Through a series of complex chemical reactions not yet fully understood, these creatures appear to be virtually immortal. None have been known to die of natural causes, causing many top medical researchers to become quite interested in studying their physiology for clues on how to prolong the lives of other living creatures, especially humans.

Cosmic Horror is one of the rare life forms found in the vacuum of space. Its diet consists of literally anything ranging from space gravel to small spacecraft. The natural habitat for Cosmic Horrors is usually deep space but there have been rare sightings recorded close to planetary bodies and even space stations.

In the distant future, an evil exists so stealth that one is unaware of how subtle and pervasive the Cthulhu effects can be on one's mind, until it is too late. Cthulhu never shows himself, but is ever at odds with those who invade his domain. His focus is to inhabit your every thought until you go mad.

You must be of sound mind and body to be able to resist the seductive nature of his attempts, for on Monria, Cthulhu is not a creature to kill for reward, but an unfathomable and controlling energy that permeates every space.

When you think you are safe ... chances are you are not.
